Online Student Portal

Introduction: 

The Student Online Portal is a web-based application
designed to provide a convenient platform for students to
access various educational resources, interact with
instructors and peers, and manage their academic activities.
The portal aims to streamline communication and enhance
collaboration between students and educational institutions

Actors:

  • Students
  • Faculty
  • Administration
  • Parents

Modules:

  • Dashboard:

    A personalized homepage displaying important announcements, events, and quick access to frequently used modules.

  • Profile Management:

    Allow students to update their personal information, contact details, and profile pictures.

  • Course Information:

    View class schedules, syllabi, and access to course materials, including lecture notes, presentations, and assignments.

  • Grades and Transcripts:

    Check grades, academic transcripts, and GPA information.

  • Registration and Enrollment:

    Register for classes, add/drop courses, and view enrollment history.

  • Student Services:

    Request transcripts, certificates, or other administrative services.

  • Feedback and Surveys:

    Participate in surveys, provide feedback on courses and instructors.

  • Support and Helpdesk:

    Access a helpdesk or support system for technical issues or general inquiries.

  • News and Updates:

    Stay informed about campus news, events, and achievements.
